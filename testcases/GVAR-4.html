GVAR–4: Crawling Lizard

The font for this test case is Zycon. In 1993, David Berlow had designed this font for illustrating the capabilities of TrueType GX; 23 years later, the font got updated for OpenType 1.8. The new version supports a small number of Emoji such as 🦎 U+1F98E LIZARD. If your OpenType implementation is correct, the lizard should crawl. Variation axis M₁ moves its legs while axis T₁ bobs the head.
